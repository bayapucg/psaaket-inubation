ABOUT US

PSAAKET, the school of start, by Pracha Technologies started its journey in the areas of Learning and Development with a gracious ambition of providing cost effective solutions in imparting quality education to all students who dream to be a part of the ever-growing technology arena. We made a humble start and slowly envisaged upon and expanded our horizons to a formidable extent in the Technical-Learning and Development industry within short time frames. We could achieve this by providing Quality education by understanding those areas of student training - where a need is seen for enhancement of skill sets, which are in cognizance with the industry needs and standards. PSAAKET’s forte is in understanding, nurturing, motivating and honing the skill sets of the students from the scratch to the professional level.

The Vision with which PSAAKET operates is to build the concrete BRIDGE between the Academic learning and the Professional Industry and to give 100% assistance for industry placement. We understand the need of a system study, analysis and implementation of the aspects involved in student learning. This helps to make the imbibed skill sets to be practical and cognizant to the industry needs. With this commitment, we reach out to the students for result oriented and cost-effective training solutions in the areas of Soft skills and Technical skill development, Software and Live Project Training, Project management…etc through Class-room teaching sessions, lab sessions, Course Oriented Seminars/workshops, lectures by experts from the industry, etc., without compromising on the Quality and standards as expected and laid down by the Industry.

we provide training with Industry Proven standards to all academic as well as Professionals who are in the process of changing or improving their knowledge/skill sets in their current Domain, at different levels of exposure

Purpose and objective of the program

The knowledge and experience gained from exposure to the industry had given us an indebt knowledge of the needs of the industry that are lacking among the students. the know-how formed a basis for designing and developing a comprehensive program that encompasses training schedules on niche areas that are needed for career enhancement. With this goal in mind PSAAKET had designed and developed a comprehensive student training program called the “Student Incubation Program” ( SIP), with the only strategic goal: to focus on the training the students on niche areas which would hone the student on technical and soft skills thus increasing their knowledge base and enhancing their career prospects. The program is a well balanced mix of theoretical and practical (industry oriented) approaches which will focus not only on growing need of academic knowledge but also on accelerating career prospects.

There is a gap between what the industry needs, and the same with which the student is not well equipped with. The SIP will bridge this gap by empowering the student with the required skill sets, (tech and soft skills) which will make him/her complete with all the ammunition to face the career market, once out of the academic schedules. The program is designed and spread over a period of 4 years, with an option of lateral entry and midway exit. The program will be an on-campus program so that the student will not have to physically move out of the campus. This is to see that the regular academic schedules and the program are on the same page.

Each training schedule SIP uses a combination of instruction, worked examples, multimedia, case studies and group discussion which embeds the practical application of our course principles in relation to the industry needs. The modules are designed to be highly interactive and involving.

Audience

B.Tech   B.C.A.   B.Sc.   M.Tech   M.C.A   M.Sc. and (All specialisations)

Course structure

Training is application-oriented, aimed at improving and polishing those skill sets which are most applied in the performance of job. Training needs are addressed only to the extent necessary to support this focus. The program is designed to be on-campus, spread over a span of four years. Classes will be scheduled for the week-ends/holidays only. Each training day will have a 3 to 6 hour of the instruction window. Each module spreads onto all the four years, starting with the first year – on a bottom-up approach starting with the rudiments in the first year, slowly putting all the pieces together and completing the bigger picture at the end of the fourth year. Each module is carefully designed to be of certain hours of duration during the entire program. The program includes

Class-room instruction

Lab sessions

Group discussions

Workshops/Seminars

Intership projects( with industry exposure)

Guest lectures from industry experts

Mentor Guided Assessment Programs
